Disclaimer:

I chose this song because in fact to me it is a comic song. Complaining about how tough love and life are. This song gave a lot of sense to me when I was young when I was much more clueless about how humans relationships work and especially so called Love. Today I am just less clueless than before but now I recognize that Love exists and it isn't a toy which can give us some quick fun even though it can have us a lot of joy, but with much more effort and different from a toy real, active and daily practiced Love, some time with a bitter taste is capable of providing I long and durable feeling of joy throughout the time..

HTML Structure:

The lyrics are wrapped in a <div> class="lyrics" > element. The title and author are displayed prominently at the top, centered for emphasis.

CSS Styling:

  • A dark background (#2c2c2c) is used to reflect the sense of disillusionment and uncertainty.
  • The container holding the lyrics has a darker grey background (#1a1a1a) with a strong shadow to give it a somber, reflective look.
  • Fonts: Arial for the body for a clean, modern look, and Impact for the title to add a strong, bold presence.
  • Colors for the title and alternating lyrics are shades of red and grey (#ff4d4d, #ff9999, #cccccc) to reflect the passion and melancholy of the song.
  • A hover effect on the lyrics adds a subtle interaction, symbolizing the fleeting and uncertain nature of emotions. This setup aims to visually represent the raw, rebellious sentiment and the underlying disillusionment with love in "Me Cago En El Amor" using a more muted color palette and strong fonts.
